Vibe Marketing Playbook

Host “Signal Sessions” on Twitter Spaces/Discord where you live-review fresh companies, invite operators to react, and let the audience vote on whether to pursue the lead—this creates participatory proof.

Ship playful artifacts: physical “Signal Cards” mailed to early champions, memeified breakdowns comparing “spray-and-pray lists vs. FundSignal receipts,” and lightweight merch that doubles as invite codes.

Encourage user-generated content by giving subscribers embeddable cards showing their weekly best lead; when RevOps leaders share them, it becomes organic endorsement.
